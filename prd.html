Product Description

This Garam Masala is sun-dried and hand-blended by women artisans using premium whole spices. It adds a warm, aromatic depth to curries, stews, marinades, and more.

  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Black pepper
  • Nutmeg
